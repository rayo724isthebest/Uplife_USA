        END Homelessness!

        Millions of people in America go homeless every year. Help us provide them with enough food and resources.

        UpLife is dedicated to providing immediate aid and sustainable solutions to homelessness. Through community partnerships, volunteer efforts, and direct assistance, we're working to ensure everyone has access to basic necessities and a path to stability.

        Join Our Mission Donate Today

        Our Mission

        Helping Homeless Shelters Meet Rising Demands

        At UpLife, our mission is to essentially; help homeless shelters meet rising demands of homelessness by providing them with as many resources as possible that allows them to do more for the homeless.

        We do this primarily, by making and donating sandwiches weekly. We hope to expand our reach of what and how we can provide by raising more awareness, members, and funds.

        Our goal is to create sustainable solutions that address the immediate needs of those experiencing homelessness while working toward long-term change.

        Our Impact

        Making a Difference Together

        Over the course of one year, we have been able to donate large amounts of sandwiches and snacks and we have hosted drink groups at the Roof Above Day Services Center as well. We have also donated many needs to Roof Above as well like socks, soap, tissues, etc.

        About Us

        Our Story

        We are a nonprofit organization striving to help homeless shelters meet rising demands of homelessness and to end poverty.

        UpLife started as a part of my journey through the NSF Youth Changemakers Journey program. Through this program I was assigned the task to look for problems in my community to get a better understanding of my community.

        Through this process, I started to see a pattern of homeless people everywhere. I knew I had to do something about it. This is when my friend and I took the initiative to start UpLife.

        To us, the idea behind this was that we have all the resources we need to survive so why should we not find a way to get these resources to those who are unable to access them as well.

        Our Team

        Dedication. Expertise. Passion. Here at UpLife, we are a team. Everything that we are able to do is thanks to our wonderful team that values our values, missions, and goals to make the world a better place.
